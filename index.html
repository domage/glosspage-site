How it works

Set it once, translate anything instantly

Set up Back Tap or Action Button

Assign GlossPage to your iPhone's Back Tap gesture or Action Button in Settings. Takes 30 seconds — once.

Double-tap. Screenshot taken.

From any app, any screen — tap the back of your phone or press the Action Button. GlossPage captures the screen instantly.

Text found. Translation overlaid.

OCR finds every text block. The translation is placed back exactly where the original text was — no layout breaks, no copying.

Works on PDFs and web pages too

Open a PDF or tap Translate in Safari — GlossPage handles it page by page. Share, export, or save to your translation history.

Features

Everything you need to read the world

Translates screenshots, images, PDFs, and web pages. Finds text with OCR and places translated text back into the original layout. Source language is detected automatically. Your original files stay private — only recognized text blocks are sent for remote translation.

Double Tap or Action Button

Assign GlossPage to Back Tap or the Action Button. From any app, double-tap the back of your iPhone — screenshot taken, translation done.

Free · limited

Image & Screenshot Translation

Any image, any screenshot — street signs, menus, books, documents. Translated text overlays the original in place. Free includes limited daily use; GP Local unlocks unlimited local translation.

Free · limited

PDF Translation

Translate multi-page PDFs page by page, with translated text overlaid in the original layout. GP Local removes watermarks; GP Pro adds high-quality online translation and export.

Free · limited

Web Page Translation

Translate entire web pages through the Safari extension — text replaced in place, layout preserved. GP Pro unlocks unlimited Safari translation.

Free · limited

Share Extension

Translate any image from Photos, Safari, or any app — directly from the iOS Share Sheet, without opening GlossPage.
